Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Psychology, University of Virginia, under the supervision of Prof. Xin Cynthia Tong. Under her guidance, I have been working on the topic of missing data, longitudinal research, and Bayesian statistics at the Bayesian and Longitudinal Data Science Lab.
